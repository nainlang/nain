DIAGRAM FOR EXPLAINING THE FOR LOOP

DECLARATION:

[For keyword]
      ↓
[Counter variable (iterator, normally called i)]
      ↓
[How to increment the variable after each iteration]
      ↓
[Curly Braces]
      ↓
[Code to run every iteration]

WHILE RUNNING:

[do the code in the curly braces]
      ↓
[is the variable meeting the condition]
        ↓        ↓
        yes     no
        ↓        ↓
        (stop) (continue)
